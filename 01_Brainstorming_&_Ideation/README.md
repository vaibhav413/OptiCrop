# Epic 1: Problem Definition and Understanding

## Overview

This epic focuses on understanding the challenges involved in recommending suitable crops based on soil nutrients and environmental conditions. It includes gathering user and business requirements, studying existing machine learning approaches for crop recommendation, analyzing the Crop Recommendation Dataset (`Crop_recommendation.csv`), and understanding the overall impact of developing the OptiCrop system.

## Objectives

- Define the crop recommendation problem in agriculture.
- Gather user and business requirements for the OptiCrop system.
- Study existing machine learning techniques for crop recommendation.
- Analyze the Crop Recommendation Dataset (`Crop_recommendation.csv`).
- Understand the agricultural, social, and business impact of the proposed solution.

## Stories

- Problem Statement
- Business Requirements
- Literature Survey
- Dataset Analysis
- Social and Business Impact

## Deliverables

- Problem Statement Document
- Business Requirements Specification
- Literature Survey Report
- Dataset Analysis Report
- Social and Business Impact Analysis

## Success Criteria

- Clear understanding of the crop recommendation problem.
- Well-documented business and user requirements.
- Comprehensive study of existing crop recommendation systems.
- Complete analysis of the Crop Recommendation Dataset (`Crop_recommendation.csv`).
- Clearly defined project scope, assumptions, and limitations.

## Expected Outcomes

At the end of this epic, the project team will have a comprehensive understanding of the crop recommendation problem, stakeholder requirements, existing machine learning techniques, and the Crop Recommendation Dataset (`Crop_recommendation.csv`). This knowledge will provide a strong foundation for data preprocessing, model development, evaluation, and deployment of the OptiCrop web application.
